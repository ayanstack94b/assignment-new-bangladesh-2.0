Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving creative solutions for a progressive future and growth.

Sustainability

Ensuring long-term environmental balance and resource preservation.

Infrastructure

Building robust foundations for modern development and connectivity.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities to achieve their fullest potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Revolutionizing businesses with advanced technologies, enhancing efficiency, and improving customer experiences.

Green Energy

Harnessing renewable resources to power a sustainable future, reducing carbon footprints globally.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing smart cities with cutting-edge facilities, promoting innovation and enhancing connectivity.

People are not born to just make money and get rich. That is not the purpose of life. The purpose is to contribute to society, to solve problems, and to uplift the lives of others. Social business is a new way of thinking. It’s a form of business that focuses on solving social problems, not maximizing profits. We need businesses that address issues like poverty, unemployment, and inequality. It’s time to shift our focus from profit-driven motives to creating businesses that serve humanity. Every time we solve a problem in a sustainable way, we create a solution that impacts many. Social business is not a charity. It’s about creating a model that empowers people, provides them with the tools to succeed, and ultimately transforms the community for the better.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
